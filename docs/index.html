UPTIME

UPTIME is an application that logs your server/pc's up-time and the current user that is logged in.



FAQ

Why use UPTIME?


UPTIME logs your Server/PC's current up-time and who was logged into your PC. If you suspect somebody has been logging into your PC without your permission this tool will log what account was used and how long it was used for. You can also use it to see how long your server has been running exactly since login.
